Visit Kothamangalam

Enjoy the serene Thattekad Bird Sanctuary, the historic St. Thomas Church, and the beautiful Bhoothathankettu Dam in Kothamangalam

Top three activities to do in Kothamangalam

Haven for Birdwatchers

Covering an area of about 25 square kilometers, it hosts over 300 species, including rare and endangered ones, making it one of the premier bird sanctuaries in India.

Historic Syro-Malabar Catholic church

Known for its architectural beauty and religious significance, it attracts numerous visitors and pilgrims every year.

Scenic dam built across the Periyar River

Surrounded by lush forests and natural beauty, it is a popular spot for picnics, boating, and nature walks.
